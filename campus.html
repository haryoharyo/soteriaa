Campus Life

Lectures


Lectures are one of the major activities on campus. Our lectures take place in the polytechnic’s lecture rooms and laboratories that are quite close to the hostels. It is a common place to see students strolling in clusters or individually to their lecture venues.
All lectures take place within hours of 9 a.m and 4 p.m Mondays to Fridays.

Security


Our campus premises are fenced and well guarded. Students are not allowed to leave the school except in unique situations and this can only be done by obtaining exit from the school authorities, which they are to return on or before the approved time. A student that leaves the school without exit or that does not return as at when due is will face disciplinary actions.
The hostel manager monitor the students’ activities in the hostels and also provide support for them where necessary.

Hostel Accommodation


The school provides hostel accomodation for a small number of the students population. Students are reminded that provision of accomodation is a privilege and not a right, the school management can withdraw this privilege from any student deemed to have violated standing regulations. Any student, who had come before the student disciplinary committee and had been found guilty and given a letter of warning or suspension forfeits all entitlement to hostel accomodation.

Cafeteria System


The school's Cafeteria is responsible for cooking and provision of foods to the students in a very hygienic way. The cafeteria of the school oversees the activities of the cafeteria System on the campus. It is against the hostel rules and regulation for residential students to cook in their hostels of residence.

Medical Facility


There is a first aid provision for staff and student's needs in the school. In addition to this, there is a collaboration with a private hospital within the school's locality for the provision of medical need for staff and students.

Sports


Obafemi Awolowo stadium which is a stone throw from the school is being used by the school for football training and for other sporting purposes with other schools.

Religious Activities


The students are to fully participate in religious activities organized by the school to strengthen their moral/spiritual growth and development.

Other Extracurricular Activities


Students can be involved in other extracurricular activities on campus such as social groups, study groups, and so on; so far their activities do not constitute an offence to the polytechnic’s rules and regulations. soteria business school generally provides students with the freedom to interact, play and study such that their Polytechnic experience is memorable and full.

Visiting


Visitors are not allowed to go direct to student's rooms without first of all clearing with the management and signing the visitor's register. The visitor must also sign out on the departure.

Student's Unionism


Membership of student union is strictly prohibited in the school.

General behavior


Students of SBS are generally expected to behave themselves as responsible members of the society. Any student caught engaging in any form of verbal/physical combat shall be disciplined.
