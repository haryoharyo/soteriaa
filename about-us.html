Our Journey

Soteria Business School was established in 2007 under the Oyo state Ministry of Education as a vocational institution, Which provides training in Business Administration, Software and Hardware Engineering and computer studies.

The institution is under the leadership of Soteria Trust, non-governmental charity in the United Kingdom. The founder is Rev’d Andy Economides. The vision is to provide excellent education that is easily affordable for the needy and vulnerable youths. Soteria Students learn new things that will help them to enjoy life greatly. Soteria students receive the necessary foundation for strong and meaningful lives.

In 2018, the institution was licensed by the Federal Ministry of Education to operate as an innovation Enterprise Institution (IEI) under the umbrella and authorization of the National Board for Technical Education (NBTE). Soteria students that successfully acquire the two years NID Programme can proceed to any polythenic for HND or have direct entry to university in Nigeria. The accredited academic programs are:-

  • Computer software Engineering

  • Computer hardware Engineering

  • Business informatics(Business management and information technology)


The school also has full time/weekend short-term programs as listed below:-

  • Desktop publishing and data processing (3 months)

  • Web design (3 months)

  • Software Engineering (3 months)

  • Computer Hardware and Networking (3 months)

  • CCTV Installation (1 month)

  • Video and Audio Editing (3 months)

  • Graphics and design (3 months)
